About this page

This website picks a random photo and quote from David Byrne, the lead singer and guitarist of the band Talking Heads! Born in Scotland and raised in America, he formed Talking Heads with Chris Frantz and Tina Weymouth in 1975. Along with Jerry Harrison, they would continue playing together until they broke up in 1991. They would later be inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2002!
